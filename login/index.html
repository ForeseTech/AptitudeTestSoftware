Aptitude Test
Please use your SVCE affiliated E-Mail ID only.

Rules:-

1. The duration of the test is 40 minutes.
2. There a total of 50 questions in the test.
3. There are 20 core departmental questions, 10 verbal ability question, 10 quantitative ability questions and 10 programming questions on the test.
4. Attempt all the questions.
5. 1 point is granted for every correct answer. There is no negative marking for wrong answers.
6. Please select your preference for online/offline mock placements. Online mock placements will be held on 20/02/2021 and 21/02/2021. Offline mock placements will be held on 27/02/2021 and 28/02/2021. You will be asked to turn up for the event only on one of the aforementioned days. The online event will be held on Zoom/Meet and you will asked to come to the college campus for the offline event. Your preference once selected cannot be changed.
